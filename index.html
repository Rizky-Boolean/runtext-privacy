Privacy Policy for RunText

Effective Date: May 24, 2024

Welcome to RunText ("we", "our", or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our iOS application.

1. Information We Collect

We do not collect, store, process, or transmit any personal data whatsoever.

RunText is a completely offline application. It does not require user registration, login, or any form of account creation. We do not collect names, email addresses, locations, or device identifiers.

2. How We Use Your Information

Since we do not collect any information, we do not use your information for any purpose.

3. Device Permissions

The application requests the following system permissions solely for its core functionality:

  • Screen Orientation Control: The app adjusts the screen to landscape mode to display the running text. This is handled locally on the device and does not record or send screen data externally.

4. Third-Party Services

We do not use any third-party analytics, advertising networks (such as Google AdMob), or social sharing SDKs. There are no third-party services that have access to your data through this app.

5. Data Security

As no data is collected or transmitted over the internet, there is no risk of data interception or breach regarding personal information.

6. Children's Privacy

The app is suitable for all ages. Because we do not collect any personal information from anyone, we are fully compliant with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) and other applicable children's privacy laws.
